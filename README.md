## Introduction

This extension enhances your YouTube Music experience by providing tools to manage your playlists effectively. It helps you identify and remove unavailable songs, ensuring your playlists are always playable.

## The Problem

Have you ever opened a YouTube Music playlist only to find that some of the songs are no longer available? This can be frustrating, especially for long playlists. Manually finding and removing these tracks is a tedious process. This extension automates that process for you.

## Features

- **Scan Playlists:** Automatically scan your playlists for unavailable tracks.
- **Remove Tracks:** Remove all unavailable tracks from a playlist with a single click.
- **Find Replacements:** Find alternative tracks for unavailable songs.

## How to Use

1.  **Open a Playlist:** Navigate to any of your playlists on music.youtube.com.
2.  **Open the Extension:** Click on the extension icon in your navigation bar or below the play icon of playlist.

    ![Screenshot of opening the extension](/images/Extension%20buttons.png)
3. **Select a playlist:** From the list of playlists that you have access to edit, choose one to edit. If you are on a plylist page, it will be auto selected.
    ![Screenshot of selecting a playlist](/images/Editable%20playlists.png)


4.  **Scan for Unavailable Tracks:** Click the "Find Unavailable Tracks" button. This will find unavailable tracks and their replacements.

    ![Screenshot of scanning a playlist](/images/Find%20and%20replace.png)

5.  **Replace Unavailable Tracks:** Once the scan is complete, click the "Replace Selected" button.

    ![Screenshot of replacing tracks](/images/Replacing.png)

6. **Other Options:** You can also choose to only remove the selected tracks, or only add the replacements.

7. **Reminder** YouTube Music identifies changes to playlist and will try to refresh the page. You have to cancel the refresh of page for the extension to work.
